Our History

Hotel Seetha stands as a welcoming haven in Kandy, a city steeped in ancient lore and royal grandeur. While the hotel itself may not boast centuries of history, its very name echoes the timeless tales of the Ramayana, connecting guests to the region's profound cultural heritage. Kandy, the last bastion of Sri Lankan kings, surrounds the hotel with stories etched in its temples and landscapes. The Temple of the Tooth Relic, a short distance away, resonates with centuries of Buddhist devotion. Hotel Seetha, therefore, positions itself not just as an accommodation, but as a gateway to exploring the historical heart of Sri Lanka, where legends and reality intertwine. Guests are invited to immerse themselves in the spirit of this storied land, finding comfort and connection within the walls of Hotel Seetha.
