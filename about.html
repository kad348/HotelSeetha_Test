About Our Hotel

Hotel Seetha, nestled in the heart of Kandy, offers a tranquil retreat amidst the city's vibrant cultural tapestry. We pride ourselves on providing our guests with warm hospitality and comfortable accommodations, creating a home away from home. Whether you're exploring the historic Temple of the Tooth Relic, wandering through the lush Royal Botanical Gardens, or simply seeking a peaceful escape, Hotel Seetha serves as the perfect base for your Kandy adventures. We are dedicated to ensuring your stay is memorable, with attentive service and a genuine Sri Lankan experience.
